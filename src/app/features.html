Sketch and Photoshop integration, you can export your designs with single click

With Draft’s Sketch and Photoshop plugins, you will be able to export your designs to Draft with a single click. Also if you want to update them, you will do that straight from Sketch or Photoshop, just hit the sync button.

No need to export your design assets anymore

With Draft, you will not need to export any of your design assetsanymore. Draft will get that after you import your designs. Developers won't ask you for any assets anymore!

Get a perfect style guide in just seconds

Once your import all your screens, you will get a perfect style guide that can be downloaded or shared with anyone with a public link or you can change the link privacy.

Get instant feedback on your designs from your team members

Anyone on your team can create a note on your design screens, and you can reply to any note created. Also you can mark it as resolved or leave it so you can keep track of your feedback progress.

See how your screens are linked together in simple clicks

With Draft's screen flow feature, you can create and see how your screens are linked together. Add hotspots and link screens, so the flow is mapped easily with just few clicks.

Get notified with Slack’s integration

Integrate with Slack and get notified if someone left a note, you can get it directly and automatically into your Slack channel.

No need to ask for assets, Colors or fonts

Draft will extract colors, assets, and fonts for you, not only that, it will give you not just a working code, but yet beautiful. Just copy and paste the generated code and everything is ready.

No more hassle with assets sizes

With Draft you will get those assets extracted in all of the possible sizes, and you will get the code to be using the correct version depending on the platform. No hard time managing all of the versions anymore!

Too many assets to manage with all styles and weights, no more

Inspecting every single element style or font weight and size for every paragraph in the screen you are implementing is a time wasting task. Now with Draft you can focus on more important things while Draft will get you all of that perfectly in peices of code that you can use instantly.

Hard time measuring the elements and doing stuff that is out of your focus

Now it's very easy to get any size, measurement, alignment, or distance. Just use the guides that are provided with each screen element by clicking on it. Get the measurements straight away, very easily!

Easily communicate with designers by adding notes

You can now add some notes on specific spots on the designed screens. Designers can check and reply to you on these notes, no more hard times describing a portion of the screen that you want to be tweaked or you have a question about. Mark these notes as resolved if you finished with them, or leave them so you can keep track of your feedback progress.

Get instant updates on design changes on slack

If you use Slack, you can integrate Draft to send you about any updates to any screen in the project. Just customize it to send to a specific channel, and get that updates instantly!
